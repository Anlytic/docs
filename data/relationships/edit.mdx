# How to edit or remove a data relationship?
In this page we are going to walk you over how you can edit or remove a data relationships.

1. Go to `settings`. Click on `sources`. Click on `edit`. Click on `relationships`.


2. To remove a relationship, hover over the relationship line. Click on the `'x'` on the line to remove the relationship. Click on `save & continue` to save your changes.
